I've Sat in Your Chair

I've spent more than 30 years building businesses and leading teams through the kinds of challenges that don't come with a playbook.

My executive career spans roles as a founder, divisional leader, CTO, COO and CEO. I've led global organizations through acquisitions, economic downturns, AI adoption and operational meltdowns. I've made the 3am decisions that couldn't wait until morning and walked into rooms where everyone expected answers I didn't have yet.

I've stared at a revenue forecast that dropped 25% overnight and addressed a company of 3,200 people with honesty instead of spin. I've flown to a client site for a 72-hour crisis recovery. I've built a business from a whiteboard pitch into a division that generated half the company's revenue.

Today, I partner with Boards, CEOs, founders, and executive teams at $20M–$1B companies who are navigating growth, transformation, or transition. My fractional executive practice provides senior-level strategic guidance without the commitment or cost of a full-time hire.

Why Dualign

The name says it all: Dual + Align. Leadership demands two forces that often feel at odds: the people instincts to build trust, retain talent, and lead through uncertainty, and the analytical rigor to make hard decisions, optimize performance, and drive results. Most leaders lean one way or the other. The best learn to align both.

That's what I help you do. I work with executives to integrate culture-driven leadership with analytical rigor, not as competing priorities, but as complementary strengths. When heart and head work together, you build organizations where people thrive and the numbers work.

How I Work
