The Heart & Head quadrant maps leadership effectiveness across two dimensions. When both Heart and Head are strong, organizations operate in the High-Performance Zone—decisions are fast, talent stays, and transformation sticks. High Heart with low Head produces culture-strong but systems-weak teams—engaged people without execution discipline. High Head with low Heart produces systems-strong but culture-fragile organizations—efficient operations that bleed top talent. When both are low, organizations enter the Fracture Zone—slow decisions, high attrition, and stalled transformation.
